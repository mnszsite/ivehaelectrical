About Us

IVEHA Electrical, established on June 17, 2020, is dedicated to providing high-quality electrical solutions across Papua New Guinea. Our experienced team ensures reliability, safety, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Our History

Founded with the intention to offer the best electrical engineering and technical services, IVEHA Electrical has tackled numerous projects for both business houses and government organizations in Papua New Guinea. Our strong team of experts ensures quality and reliability in every task.
