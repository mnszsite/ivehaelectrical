About Us

IVEHA Electrical, established on June 17, 2020, is dedicated to providing high-quality electrical solutions across Papua New Guinea. Our experienced team ensures reliability, safety, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Our History

Founded with the intention to offer the best electrical engineering and technical services, IVEHA Electrical has tackled numerous projects for both business houses and government organizations in Papua New Guinea. Our strong team of experts ensures quality and reliability in every task.

Our Goal

To be a respectable Electrical Contractor delivering beyond expectations.

Provide job opportunities for Papua New Guineans with better salary packages to help them sustain their living.

To contribute to the Country’s vision of electrifying the rest of Papua New Guinea.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions, and deliver quality work within a reasonable time frame. We believe in the importance of reliability, professionalism, and innovation in our work.
