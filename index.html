About Us

IVEHA Electrical, established in 2020, provides high-quality electrical solutions across Papua New Guinea. We specialize in electrical design, installation, renewable energy, and safety compliance.

Service Specialities

Electrical Design & Installation

We offer comprehensive electrical design services, ensuring that installations meet both functional and aesthetic needs. Our team is skilled in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, providing tailored solutions to suit your requirements.

Renewable Energy Solutions

We specialize in solar energy systems and other renewable technologies, helping clients reduce energy consumption and lower their carbon footprint. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to provide innovative energy-efficient solutions.

Electrical Safety Compliance

Ensuring that all installations adhere to local regulations and standards is paramount. We conduct thorough safety assessments, provide electrical installation condition reports, and implement compliance measures to protect your property and ensure peace of mind.

Our Projects

Project 1 - Goroka Airport Tower

We have a 2-year contract with NiuSky Pacific to do the following tasks at Goroka Airport Tower Facility:

  1. Two Weekly Inspections on the Standby Generator, UPS, Air Conditioning Unit, and the Electrical Power System.
  2. Service the Standby Generator when it's due for service.
  3. Be on Standby for Call-out or breakdown work.
  4. Identify any issue on the power system and repair or maintain.
  5. Other electrical installation work.

Project 2 - Kainantu Street Lighting

Professionally provided Eastern Highlands Provincial Government with inspections, scope of works, and costing for Kainantu streetlight project.

  1. Carry out physical inspection and count the number of PNG power posts in Kainantu.
  2. Create dialogue with PNG Power on the said project.
  3. Compile a scope of works including costings and work schedule.
  4. Assist in Contractor selection and project supervision.

Project 3 - School Electrical Installations

We were subcontracted by Gasowo Gehani Construction to do full electrical installation on 2 newly built 2 Story Double Classrooms for North Goroka Jnr High School.

  1. Load Demand Calculation.
  2. Circuit Breaker and Cable Sizing.
  3. Material Listing and Scope of Work.
  4. Full installation based on ASNZS 3000.
